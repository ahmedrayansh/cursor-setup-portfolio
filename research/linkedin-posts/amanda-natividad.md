## Post 2

Date: 2025-01-16  
Topic: Zero-click marketing definition  
Source: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_zero-click-marketing-its-a-term-youve-activity-7285806355725135873-jCM3

Summary: Amanda defines zero-click marketing as creating value where people already spend time, without depending on referral clicks. She points to zero-click search, platform incentives that reduce outbound reach, and the limits of link-driven digital PR.

Research takeaway: For LinkedIn organic, the post itself needs to carry the value. The link can be additive, but the reader should understand the main idea without leaving the feed.

## Post 3

Date: 2024-07-09  
Topic: Optimizing for impressions instead of website traffic  
Source: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_but-if-i-do-zero-click-marketing-how-will-activity-7216456099938410496-h7dO

Summary: Amanda clarifies that zero-click marketing does not mean never sending people to a website. It means accepting that platforms limit outbound traffic, so brands should optimize for impressions, engagement, memory, and trust until buyers seek them out later.

Research takeaway: The right LinkedIn KPI may not be click volume. For B2B SaaS, impressions, quality comments, direct search, self-reported attribution, and remembered expertise may be better indicators.
