## Key Points

- AI search is changing how buyers discover and validate brands, but the threat is often overstated or misunderstood.
- SEO measurement needs to move beyond raw traffic, rankings, and click-through rates.
- Authority, citations, brand visibility, commercial-intent demand, and pipeline are increasingly important.

## Notes

### Executive Anxiety Around AI Search

The webinar is framed for executives and boards asking for an "AI SEO strategy." Gaetano's practical value is cutting through vague AI panic and explaining what is actually changing in organic discovery.

### Measurement After Click Collapse

As AI Overviews and LLM answers reduce clicks, traffic alone becomes a weaker measure of organic impact. Gaetano's LinkedIn posts pair well with this webinar: track AI visibility, citations, brand search, self-reported attribution, and organic pipeline instead.

### Authority as an AI Search Asset

If AI systems decide which brands to cite or recommend, then authority is not just a traditional ranking factor. It becomes a visibility and trust asset across search, AI tools, review sites, media, and communities.

## Research Takeaway

Gaetano's material is useful for connecting LinkedIn organic and SEO. Both channels now create influence that may not be directly attributed, so B2B SaaS teams need a measurement model that includes brand demand and buyer memory.
