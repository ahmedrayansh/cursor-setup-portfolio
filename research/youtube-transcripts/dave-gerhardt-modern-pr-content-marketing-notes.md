# Dave Gerhardt - Podcast Notes

Video Title: Life Is Too Short to Work For a CEO Who Doesn't Get Marketing
Link: https://creators.spotify.com/pod/profile/b2brevenuevitals/episodes/104---Life-Is-Too-Short-to-Work-For-a-CEO-Who-Doesnt-Get-Marketing--Dave-Gerhardt-eqan7j

## Key Points
- B2B SaaS brands should think like media companies, not only campaign teams.
- Modern PR includes founder voice, audience building, content marketing, podcasts, and community presence.
- Attribution often starts imperfectly; early organic brand work may need conviction before it is fully measurable.

## Transcript Notes

### Modern PR as Organic Distribution
The episode description frames the conversation around B2B influencer marketing, content marketing trends, modern PR, attribution, and buying intent. For LinkedIn strategy, the useful takeaway is that PR is not limited to press mentions. A founder or executive can use consistent public content to become known by the market.

### Being the Media Company
Dave's broader strategy is to stop waiting for media coverage and become the source buyers already follow. For B2B SaaS, this means publishing useful ideas, customer lessons, opinions, and examples regularly enough that the company becomes a trusted resource.

### Attribution and Conviction
The episode notes that attribution can begin with a gut feeling. Organic LinkedIn often influences buyers before they fill out a form, so the strategy should be measured with a mix of direct feedback, self-reported attribution, inbound quality, and sales conversations.
