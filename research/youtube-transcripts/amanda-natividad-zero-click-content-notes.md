## Transcript Notes

### Platform-Native Value
Amanda's zero-click idea is especially relevant to LinkedIn because the platform rewards in-feed consumption and often reduces reach on outbound links. B2B SaaS teams should make the post itself useful, not just a teaser for a blog.

### Repurpose Existing Long-Form Content
The MicroConf description notes that Amanda shares methods for creating zero-click content from long-form assets. A SaaS team can turn webinars, interviews, product research, and customer conversations into LinkedIn posts that stand alone.

### Trust Before Traffic
The strategy is not anti-website. It is a recognition that buyers often form opinions before clicking. LinkedIn content should create memory, authority, and confidence so the buyer searches for the brand later.
